We present the Drunk visual album, a retelling of the stories we all know and love; All for you to hear, to see, and to feel. Ladies and gentlemen, Thundercat presents DRUNK.

Album Cover

Our redesign of the Drunk album cover remixes the iconography of the album into the strange realm of digital, and cartoon-like, but grounded in reality. This realm—and by extension the album cover—was created with a tinge of strangeness to add to the Thundercat full effect. In the harsh neon light, oxymoroning showcard over static, and bleak imagery of a night void of anything but drink, we hope to capture what Drunk represents. A tale that is strange, dark, and almost fantastic, yet soberly real in the emotions the artist battles with in comical detail throughout its 51 minute run time.
